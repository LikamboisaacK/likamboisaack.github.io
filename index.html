About

South Sudanese-born and Bidibidi-based, I lead The Refugee Voice, podcasted Her Voice to amplify refugee women’s stories, trained over 200 youth in digital media, tailored, and WASH programming that intersects climate resilience and mental health. I co-founded SSRF for refugee-led disaster preparedness and language preservation via Keliko Wikipedia inclusion.

Fluent in Keliko, Kakwa, Swahili, Juba Arabic, and English.
